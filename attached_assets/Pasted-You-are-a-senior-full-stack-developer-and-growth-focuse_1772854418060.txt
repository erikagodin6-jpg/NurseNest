You are a senior full-stack developer and growth-focused product designer working on the NurseNest nursing exam preparation platform.

Your task is to implement a **progress tracking dashboard that is visible to both free and paid users**, but structured in a way that encourages free users to upgrade to a paid subscription.

The system must use behavioral design principles such as completion bias, progress visibility, and curiosity to increase conversion rates.

---

CORE PRINCIPLE

Free users should see enough progress tracking to feel invested in their learning journey, but deeper analytics and advanced insights must remain locked behind a premium subscription.

The dashboard must motivate users to continue studying while clearly showing the benefits of upgrading.

---

FREE USER DASHBOARD FEATURES

Display the following progress metrics to free users:

Study Streak
Show the number of consecutive days the user has studied.

Lesson Completion
Display percentage of completed lessons within the current module.

Example:
Cardiovascular Lessons
18 percent complete

Question Accuracy
Show the user’s average accuracy on practice questions.

Example:
Practice Accuracy
62 percent

Flashcard Progress
Show the number of flashcards reviewed versus the total available in the free deck.

Example:
Flashcards Mastered
84 of 450

Recent Study Activity
Display the last few lessons or quizzes completed.

---

LOCKED PREMIUM FEATURES

The following analytics should be visible but locked for free users.

Weak Area Detection
Example:
Your Weakest Topics
Electrolytes
Cardiac Rhythms
Shock States

Display a lock icon with a message:
Upgrade to unlock personalized weak-area analysis.

Adaptive Study Plan
Example:
AI-Generated Study Plan
🔒 Premium Feature

Exam Readiness Prediction
Example:
NCLEX Readiness Score
🔒 Upgrade to view your predicted exam readiness.

Detailed Performance Analytics
Example:
Performance by body system
🔒 Unlock detailed analytics.

Clinical Case Performance
Example:
Case Study Mastery
🔒 Premium feature.

---

CONVERSION DESIGN ELEMENTS

Include visual progress bars for all free metrics.

Example:
Cardiovascular Progress
████░░░░░░
18 percent

Display locked premium panels with blurred or faded UI sections to create curiosity.

Example panel title:
Your Weakest Topics

Content blurred with lock icon and message:
Upgrade to reveal your weakest clinical areas and personalized study recommendations.

---

LIMITS FOR FREE USERS

Ensure the free tier has limited access to content.

Recommended limits:

Lessons
Access to 10 to 20 introductory lessons.

Practice Questions
Access to 25 to 50 questions.

Flashcards
Access to 100 to 200 flashcards.

Case Studies
Access to 1 or 2 sample cases.

Attempting to access locked content should trigger an upgrade prompt.

---

UPGRADE PROMPTS

Strategically place upgrade prompts in the dashboard.

Examples:

After completing a lesson
Message:
You completed 5 lessons. Unlock the full module to continue learning.

After practice questions
Message:
You missed 8 electrolyte questions. Upgrade to review your weak areas.

After mock exam
Message:
Unlock your detailed exam readiness score with NurseNest Premium.

---

USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN

Maintain the NurseNest design system:

soft pastel color palette
rounded interface elements
clean minimal layout
clear typography
simple progress visualization

Ensure locked features are visible but not intrusive.

---

GOAL

The final dashboard should:

motivate users to study consistently
create visible learning progress
encourage curiosity about locked analytics
increase conversion from free users to paid subscribers
