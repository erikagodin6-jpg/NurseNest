WHY OTHER OPTIONS ARE INCORRECT REQUIREMENT

The explanations for incorrect answers must be specific to the option.

Do not use generic filler like:
- “Although this option may seem clinically reasonable...”
- “It does not represent the priority nursing action...”

Instead write option-specific reasoning.

Example for hyperkalemia question:
A. Furosemide
- can help remove potassium through the kidneys, but it does not immediately stabilize the cardiac membrane in severe hyperkalemia
B. Sodium Polystyrene
- removes potassium more slowly and is not the immediate first-line option to protect the heart
D. Insulin/Dextrose
- shifts potassium into cells effectively, but calcium gluconate is prioritized first when the question asks what protects the heart

This is the level of specificity required.
