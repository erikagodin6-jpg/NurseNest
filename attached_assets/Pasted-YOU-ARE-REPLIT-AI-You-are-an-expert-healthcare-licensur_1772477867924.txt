C) QUESTION ITEM WRITING STANDARDS (QUALITY GATES)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Every question must include:
1) Unique stem (no duplicates / near duplicates across bank).
2) Clear patient context where relevant (age, setting, cues).
3) One best answer (unless multi-select).
4) Rationales:
   - rationaleLong MINIMUM 350 words for standard questions
   - MINIMUM 600 words for CaseSet questions OR difficulty 4–5
   - Must include:
     a) Why correct is correct
     b) Why each distractor is wrong (or why not best)
     c) Clinical reasoning pathway
     d) “Exam tip / common trap”
5) Tagging:
   - careerType
   - blueprintCategory
   - subtopic
   - difficulty
   - questionType
   - cognitiveLevel: recall | application | analysis
   - is_free boolean
   - countryMode
6) No unsafe instructions. Keep educational framing.

“CAT-like” behavior requirement:
Even if exams are not true CAT, implement an adaptive practice mode:
- start difficulty 3
- adjust difficulty +/-1 based on rolling accuracy
- enforce blueprint weights
- avoid repeating recent questions
- produce a readinessScore
