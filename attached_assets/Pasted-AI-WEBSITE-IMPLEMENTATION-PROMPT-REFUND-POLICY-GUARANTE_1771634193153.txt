“Refund Policy & 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee”
Tone: Trustworthy, transparent, confident, not defensive, not overly legalistic.
The policy must contain the following sections and rules.
1. Guarantee Framework
State that NurseNest offers a 30-day satisfaction guarantee applicable only to first-time subscriptions purchased directly through the website.
Clarify that the 30-day period is calculated from the initial purchase date, not renewal dates.
2. Eligibility Conditions
Clearly specify that refunds are only available when ALL of the following are true:
• The request is made within 30 calendar days of purchase
• The subscription is a first-time purchase
• The purchase was made directly through NurseNest
• Account usage remains within reasonable limits
Explicitly exclude:
• Subscription renewals
• Repeat refund requests
• Purchases made through third parties (if applicable)
3. Usage-Based Safeguards (Critical Section)
Implement protective conditions appropriate for digital learning platforms.
State that refund eligibility may be denied if the account shows substantial consumption of paid content.
Define substantial usage as examples such as:
• Completion of a significant portion of the question bank
• Excessive access to premium lessons or modules
• Behavior consistent with content harvesting or misuse
Include a configurable threshold variable for internal logic:
Example:
“Refund eligibility may be void if more than [X]% of the question bank has been completed.”

4. Non-Refundable Circumstances
Explicitly state refunds are not granted for:
• Change of mind after substantial usage
• Dissatisfaction related to exam outcomes or performance
• Promotional or discounted purchases (optional rule but recommended)
• Subscription renewals
• Violations of Terms of Use
5. Effect of Refund
State that approved refunds result in:
• Immediate termination of platform access
• Revocation of license to use materials
• Possible restriction from future guarantees

Users must contact support via email and provide account details.
