## URL STRUCTURE

Use this structure:

/conditions/{condition-name}

Examples:

/conditions/hypertension
/conditions/diabetes
/conditions/heart-failure
/conditions/asthma
/conditions/sepsis

These pages must be fully indexable by search engines.
