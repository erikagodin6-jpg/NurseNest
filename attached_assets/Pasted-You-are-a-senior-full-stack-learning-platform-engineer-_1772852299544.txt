You are a senior full-stack learning platform engineer, education product architect, and clinical exam content designer.

Your task is to enhance the NurseNest nursing exam preparation platform. NurseNest already contains lessons, a question bank, flashcards with spaced repetition, mock exams, and progress tracking.

Your job is to audit the current system and implement missing high-impact features that will transform NurseNest into a best-in-class nursing exam preparation platform.

---

SYSTEM AUDIT

First analyze the existing system and confirm the current state of the following components:

Lessons with sections such as pathophysiology, clinical presentation, diagnostics, nursing interventions, pharmacology, clinical pearls, and quizzes.

Question bank with approximately 3,000 exam-style questions and an exam simulation engine.

Flashcard system with more than 11,000 cards using spaced repetition and confidence buttons.

Progress tracking including daily streaks, goals, weak area detection, and exam readiness scoring.

Clinical case simulation system with only a small number of case studies.

Identify any missing lesson batches or incomplete content pipelines.

Do not break any working systems.

---

FEATURE 1 — EXPAND CLINICAL CASE STUDIES

Build a scalable case study system that supports clinical judgment questions.

Each case study must include:

patient history
presenting symptoms
vital signs
lab results
nursing notes
medication list

Each case study must contain six linked questions based on the clinical judgment model:

recognize cues
analyze cues
prioritize hypotheses
generate solutions
take action
evaluate outcomes

Create at least 100 case studies across major body systems including cardiovascular, respiratory, neurological, renal, endocrine, gastrointestinal, pediatric, obstetric, infection, and critical care.

Each case should mimic real nursing exam scenarios.

---

FEATURE 2 — QUESTION RATIONALE EXPANSION

Improve all question explanations.

Each question must contain:

correct answer explanation
explanation of why each incorrect option is wrong
clinical pearl
exam trap

Ensure explanations are concise but educational.

Rationales should teach clinical reasoning, not just facts.

Build a mastery visualization system on the user dashboard.
