Implement an Exam Blueprint SEO architecture across the NurseNest platform to dramatically increase organic search traffic and properly connect marketing pages with practice content.

OBJECTIVE

Build a structured content hierarchy aligned with licensing exam blueprints such as REx-PN, NCLEX-RN, and allied health certifications.

The architecture must follow this hierarchy:

Exam
Category
Topic
Lesson
Practice Questions

STEP 1

Create exam blueprint hub pages.

Examples:

/rexpn-exam-blueprint
/nclex-exam-blueprint
/nclex-rn-exam-blueprint
/allied-health-exam-blueprint

Each blueprint page should contain:

exam overview
exam structure
number of questions
exam categories
study strategies
links to category pages

STEP 2

Create category pages based on the exam frameworks.

Examples for REx-PN:

/rexpn-safety-and-infection-control
/rexpn-health-promotion
/rexpn-psychosocial-integrity
/rexpn-physiological-integrity

Examples for NCLEX:

/nclex-pharmacology
/nclex-reduction-of-risk
/nclex-management-of-care

Each category page should include:

topic explanations
internal links to topic guides
links to practice questions
related flashcards and lessons

STEP 3

Create topic cluster pages.

Examples:

/sepsis-nursing-interventions
/ventilator-management-nursing
/hemodynamic-monitoring-nursing
/fluid-electrolyte-imbalance-nursing
/diabetes-nursing-management

Each topic page should include:

H1 title
introduction
clinical explanation
key nursing interventions
related medications
practice questions preview
internal links to lessons
internal links to flashcards

STEP 4

Implement preview question funnels.

Topic pages should link to preview question pages instead of the internal test bank.

Example:

/preview/sepsis
/preview/ventilator-management
/preview/diabetes-nursing

Preview pages should display:

5–10 preview questions
truncated rationales
locked questions below preview
CTA to unlock full question bank

STEP 5

Protect the test bank.

The route

/test-bank

must require authentication and subscription verification.

Unauthenticated users should be redirected to login or pricing.

STEP 6

Add structured FAQ sections to topic pages.

Example questions:

What is sepsis in nursing?
What interventions do nurses perform for sepsis?
How does sepsis appear on the NCLEX?
What labs indicate sepsis?

STEP 7

Add internal linking between related pages.

Example:

Ventilator management pages link to ICU nursing pages.

Sepsis pages link to infection control pages.

Fluid electrolyte pages link to renal nursing pages.

STEP 8

Add CTAs to all topic pages.

Examples:

Start Practice Questions
Explore Flashcards
View Clinical Lessons

STEP 9

Ensure all guide pages route to preview pages rather than the internal test bank.

STEP 10

Verify that all preview funnels lead to the subscription paywall before allowing full question bank access.

EXPECTED RESULT

The NurseNest platform becomes a structured clinical knowledge network aligned with licensing exam blueprints.

Topic cluster pages rank in Google for thousands of nursing search queries.

Visitors enter the site through clinical guides, explore preview questions, and convert into paid users to unlock the full question bank.
