Create system-specific hotspot click-and-identify activities for the NurseNest platform.

You must generate TWO categories of hotspot activities:

CATEGORY 1 — FREE TIER (Foundational / Pre-Nursing / Early Nursing Level)
CATEGORY 2 — PAID TIER (Clinical Assessment / Examination / Interpretation Levela)

All visuals must follow a medically accurate, professional anatomy diagram style consistent with a clean pastel clinical NurseNest aesthetic.

GLOBAL VISUAL RULES (apply to ALL diagrams):

• Diagrams must be anatomically accurate and clinically realistic
• No cartoonish or symbolic organs
• Clean medical illustration style
• Soft pastel clinical palette with good contrast
• White/light background
• Clear structure visibility
• Correct proportions and positioning

HOTSPOT DESIGN RULES (apply to ALL activities):

• Click regions must map precisely to correct anatomical structures
• Hotspots must not overlap ambiguously
• Each hotspot must include:
  - Structure name
  - Short educational explanation (1–2 sentences)
  - Optional hint text
• Activities must test recognition and understanding, not random clicking
