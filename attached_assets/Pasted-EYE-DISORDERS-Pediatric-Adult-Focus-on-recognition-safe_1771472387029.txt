EYE DISORDERS (Pediatric & Adult)
Focus on recognition + safety + functional impact.
✅ Conjunctivitis (VERY HIGH YIELD)
Extremely common exam topic.
Types (conceptual only):
• Viral
• Bacterial
• Allergic
Recognition:
⭐ Redness
⭐ Discharge differences
⭐ Contagion considerations
Exam themes:
🚨 Infection control
🚨 Hygiene education
🚨 School/daycare implications (peds)
✅ Cataracts (Adult / Geriatric High Yield)
Recognition:
• Gradual vision decline
• Cloudy vision
Safety focus:
🚨 Fall risk
🚨 Functional impairment
✅ Glaucoma (IMPORTANT)
Core concept:
⭐ Increased intraocular pressure → optic nerve damage
Recognition:
• Visual disturbances
• Eye pain (acute angle closure)
Medication safety often tested.
✅ Retinal Detachment (Classic Exam Red Flag)
Recognition:
🚨 Sudden visual change
🚨 “Flashes / floaters”
🚨 Curtain-like vision loss
Immediate referral logic.
✅ Macular Degeneration (Common Adult Topic)
• Central vision loss
• Safety implications
✅ Foreign Body / Eye Injury
Very exam-friendly.
Priorities:
🚨 Do NOT apply pressure
🚨 Protect eye
