What makes Canva feel like Canva

Canva’s “polish” comes from 6 things:

Clear workspace framing (page centered, subtle background, zoom controls)

Left rail = modes, not buttons (Templates / Elements / Text / Uploads / Brand / AI)

Right panel = properties that feel “design-native” (font, spacing, alignment, colors, effects)

Snap + guides + smart alignment (magnet snapping, spacing hints, equal distribution)

Brand kit + styles (saved colors, typography presets, component styles)

Preview + export pipeline (thumbnail previews, watermark previews, download UX)
