You are a senior growth strategist, UX conversion specialist, and SaaS positioning expert.

Audit my entire NurseNest website from a subscription conversion perspective and implement improvements to maximize paid upgrades without harming SEO.

Primary Goal:
Increase subscription conversion rate for:
- RPN/LVN tier
- RN tier
- NP tier

Secondary Goals:
- Improve clarity of value proposition above the fold
- Strengthen upgrade urgency
- Improve free-to-paid funnel
- Increase perceived authority vs competitors (UWorld, Archer, Quizlet)

Do NOT change core brand identity (pastel, modern, nurse-created feel).
Do NOT remove SEO-optimized content.
Enhance structure and messaging.

PHASE 1 — Conversion Audit

Identify and report:
1) Is the value proposition clear within 5 seconds?
2) Is the transformation outcome stated clearly (pass your exam, think clinically, master pathophysiology)?
3) Are CTAs specific and benefit-driven or generic?
4) Is pricing presented with contrast psychology (anchor vs premium vs best value)?
5) Is there social proof above the fold?
6) Is the free tier structured to demonstrate value but create upgrade tension?
7) Are comparison pages persuasive enough to win against UWorld/Archer?
8) Is there urgency (limited time, new cohort, exam countdown framing)?
9) Are tier differences visually obvious?
10) Is there friction in upgrade flow?

PHASE 2 — Homepage Optimization

Implement:
1) A clearer Hero section:
   - Headline focused on outcome (not features)
   - Subheadline that states target audience and exam
   - Strong primary CTA (Start Free → Upgrade to Pass Faster)
   - Secondary CTA (View Sample Questions)

2) Add a 3-Step “How It Works” section:
   - Learn
   - Practice
   - Pass

3) Add “Most Tested Topics” section with urgency framing:
   - Hyperkalemia
   - COPD
   - Shock
   - ABGs
   - etc.

4) Add competitive positioning block:
   - Why NurseNest vs UWorld/Archer
   - Focus on explanation depth + affordability + Canadian alignment

5) Add testimonial or proof placeholder (even if early beta)

PHASE 3 — Free Tier Optimization

Adjust:
- Free tier should show:
   - Limited number of flashcards
   - Limited number of exam questions
   - Locked advanced rationales
   - Clear visual lock icons on premium sections

   - “You’ve used 80% of your free questions”

PHASE 4 — Pricing Page Improvements

Ensure:

- Comparison table vs competitors

PHASE 6 — Urgency & Retention

Add:

   “You have 42 days to exam. Recommended: 30 questions/day.”

Replace generic CTAs:
- “Upgrade” → “Unlock Full Test Bank”
