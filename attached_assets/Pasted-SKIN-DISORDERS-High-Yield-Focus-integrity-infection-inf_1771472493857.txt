🌿 HOLISTIC / NATURAL PRODUCTS / SUPPLEMENTS (SMART & TESTABLE)
This is excellent for NurseNest differentiation and clinically realistic.
Exams increasingly include safety considerations.
✅ Critical Global Principle
Natural ≠ safe
Supplements can cause serious interactions
✅ Common High-Risk Supplements
⭐ St. John’s Wort (VERY IMPORTANT)
Interactions:
🚨 Reduces effectiveness of many drugs
🚨 Serotonin syndrome risk with SSRIs
⭐ Ginkgo Biloba
Risks:
🚨 Increased bleeding risk
🚨 Dangerous with anticoagulants
⭐ Garlic Supplements
Risks:
🚨 Bleeding risk
⭐ Ginseng
Risks:
• BP effects
• Hypoglycemia potential
⭐ Valerian Root
Risks:
🚨 CNS depression
🚨 Additive sedation with meds
⭐ Kava
Risks:
🚨 Hepatotoxicity
✅ Electrolyte / Cardiac Interaction Themes
Many supplements alter:
• Potassium
• BP
• Coagulation
Which creates exam-style safety questions.
