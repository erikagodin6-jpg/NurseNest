NURSENEST MASSIVE SEO TRAFFIC ENGINE

You are implementing a large-scale SEO architecture for NurseNest designed to generate massive organic traffic from Google searches by targeting nursing exam preparation queries.

The goal is to build a structured content system that can realistically bring tens of thousands of nursing students to the platform each month.

STACK
Frontend: React (Vite)
Backend: Express
Database: existing project database

---

## PRIMARY GOAL

Create a scalable SEO page structure that targets the highest search volume nursing education queries while integrating NurseNest practice questions, lessons, and study tools.

Every SEO page must:

• provide high educational value
• include practice questions
• include internal links
• encourage user engagement
• encourage account creation
