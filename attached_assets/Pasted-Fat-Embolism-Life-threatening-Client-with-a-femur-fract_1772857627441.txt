Fat Embolism
-	Life threatening 
-	Client with a femur fracture is at risk 
-	Globules of fat leave the bone and travel through the bloodstream to the lungs skin and brain and cause damage by occluding small vessels
-	Altered mental status will result from blocked blood vessels in the brain 
-	Embolism to the lung would result in respiratory distress
-	Hallmark sign is petechiae across the chest, axillae, and soft palate

Creatinine Clearance
-	Measure of glomerular function and is a sensitive indicator of renal disease progression. A timed urine collection measures substances excreted over time 
-	For a 24 hour collection, the first urine specimen is discarded and the time is noted. All urine for the next 24 hours is collected in a container and kept cool. At the end the client should void one last time and add the specimen to the container. 
-	Blood is drawn to measure the serum creatinine level in addition to the urine creatinine


Hyperemesis Gravidarum
-	Features: weight loss, poor skin turgor, dry mucous membranes, hypotension, tachycardia
-	lab abnormalities: hypokalemia and hyponatremia, ketonuria, increased urine specific gravity, hemoconcentration, metabolic alkalosis 


Newborn Assessment
-	decreased muscle tone must be reported
-	sacral dimples with or without skin tags are associated with spina bifida
-	single umbilical artery can be associated with congenital defects of the kidneys and heart

Cellulitis
-	keep extremity elevated
-	flatness can worsen edea


Polycythemia
-	elevated hemoglobin or hematocrit and results in increased blood viscosity 
-	increases the risk for thrombus formation 
-	additional interventions may be required to treat: IV hydration, partial exchange transfusion. 


Thyroidectomy
-	partial or complete removal of the thyroid to treat hyperthyroidism or cancer
-	patients require close monitoring because they are at increased risk for airway compromise due to potential neck swelling, hypocalcemia, nerve damage 
-	nursing includes:
o	assess for hypocalcemia, tingling, stridor, trousseau and Chvostek signs
o	hypocalcemia is a potential complication of thyroidectomy 
o	keep emergency airway equipment at bedside
o	maintain semi-fowler position to promove drainage 
o	assess for stridor, voice strength and quality which may indicate laryngeal nerve damage 
Meningeal irritation
-	Brudzinski sign: neck flexion elicits pain and knee/hip flexion
-	Kernig sign: leg extension on 90 degree flexed hip causes pain and resistance

Postpartum endometritis
-	Risks: cesarean delivery
-	Intraamniotic infection
-	Group b strep colonization
-	Prolonged rupture of membranes
-	Operative vaginal delivery
-	Features: fever more than 24 hours postpartum, uterine fundal tenderness, purulent lochia
-	Etiology: polymicrobial infection 
-	Treatment: clindamycin and gentamycin

Zika Virus 
-	Transmitted via mosquitos, sexual contact, infected body fluids
-	Causes viral symptoms (low grade fever, arthralgias) can cause microcephaly, developmental dysfunction, and encephalitis in babies born to zika infected women 
-	Avoid travel to areas if pregnant
-	Use DEET products 

Ascending Colostomy
-	Clarify enteric coated medications as they may not be properly absorbed
-	Identify foods that cause excess gas and odor
-	Stool will be semiliquid so no need for irrigation 
-	Increase fluid intake to prevent dehydration
-	Empty when 1/3rd full 

Amiodarone
-	Antiarrhythmic used to treat life threatening arrhythmias
-	Used only if other treatments have failed as it is toxic
-	Pulmonary toxicity: life threatening side effect that causes direct cellular damage and activation of immune response in the lungs
o	Symptoms = dry cough, pleuritic chest pain, dyspnea
o	Requires immediate intervention to prevent fatal irreversible lung damage
-	Other adverse effects: bradycardia, prolonged QT (low risk for torsades de pointes), lung nodules, hyper or hypothyroidism, hepatitis, corneal microdeposits, optic neuritis, blue gray skin, peripheral neuropathy 

Latex allergy
-	Balloons, avocados, banana, tomato may increase risk for latex allergy 

Status Elepticus 
-	Uncontrolled electrical discharge of neurons in the brain that interrupts normal function
-	Life-threatening emergency in which a client has a seizure for more than 5 minutes without adequate recovery which can lead to death from anoxia and/or hypoglycemia
-	Stopping seizure activity is the priority to reestablish normal oxygenation and preserve neuronal function 
-	Benzodiazepines (rectal diazepam, IV lorazepam) depress the CNS and help stop seizure activity, allowing patient recovery
EGD (esophagogastroduodenoscopy)
-	Passing an endoscope down the esophagus to visualize the upper GI structures
-	Perforation of the GI tract is a life threatening complication of EGD that can lead to peritonitis and sepsis 
-	Signs = increased temperature, increasing abdominal pain and tenderness, restlessness, tachycardia, tachypnea
-	The nurse should notify the health care provider immediately if a fever develops

NG tube insertion
-	Sometimes the tube can slip into the larynx or coil in the throat, which can cause coughing or gagging
-	The nurse should withdraw the tube slightly and then stop or pause while the client takes a few breaths 
-	After the coughing stops you can proceed, asking the patient to take a few sips of water 

Nitrazine test for rupture of membranes
-	Tests vaginal secretions to differentiate amniotic fluid, which is alkaline, and normal vaginal fluids or urine, which are acidic
-	Blue colour suggests probably rupture of membranes but the presence of blood or semen may result in a false positive

Cervical cap
-	Barrier method of contraception used with spermicide
-	Reusable, cup shapedcap is placed over the cervix before sexual intercourse to block sperm from the uterus 
-	Before each use the client should inspect for holes, cracks, tears
-	Should remain in place for more than six hours after sexual intercourse to allow time for sperm to die but the cap should be removed within 48 hours after intercourse to prevent toxic shock syndrome
