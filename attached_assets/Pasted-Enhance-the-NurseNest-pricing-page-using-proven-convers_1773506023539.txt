The goal is to increase subscription conversions while maintaining a clean, trustworthy academic brand.

Free pass conversion psychology

When a new user starts their free one-day pass, show a small progress indicator on the dashboard and question pages.

Example progress messages:

“You’ve completed 12 practice questions today.”
“You’re 15% through your first mock exam.”
“You’ve unlocked 3 new flashcard decks.”

When the free pass expires, trigger an upgrade prompt:

“You’ve already started preparing for your exam. Unlock full access to continue your progress.”

Include a clear upgrade button:

“Unlock Full Access”

Do not block the entire interface abruptly. Instead show the prompt after a user interaction such as finishing a question set.

Loss aversion messaging

After the free pass expires display:

“Your progress is saved. Upgrade now to keep practicing.”

Include a small progress summary:

* questions completed
* flashcards studied
* lessons viewed

People are more likely to upgrade when they feel they will lose existing progress.
