QUESTION BANK SYSTEM

Generate 500 questions for each profession.

Each question must include:
- question stem
- four answer choices
- correct answer
- detailed rationale for the correct answer
- detailed rationale for each incorrect answer

IMPORTANT:
- include rationales for all standard question bank questions
- do NOT include rationales on CAT exams
- rationales should be educational and detailed, not one-line explanations
- rationale target length: approximately 80 to 150 words total, with enough detail to teach
- focus on clinical reasoning, prioritization, safety, interpretation, procedures, pathophysiology, and professional workflow where appropriate
