You are a senior product designer, full-stack developer, and learning platform architect.

Your task is to build the next major development phase of the NurseNest nursing education platform.

NurseNest already contains a large library of structured nursing lessons.

The next development phase must transform the platform into a complete exam-preparation system that combines lessons, questions, exam simulations, and adaptive study tools.

The goal is to create a product that competes with premium exam platforms used by nursing students preparing for licensing exams.

------------------------------------------------

PRODUCT CONTEXT

NurseNest is a nursing education platform designed to help students prepare for licensing exams.

Supported learner tiers:

RPN
RN
NP

The platform includes:

lesson content
study guides
practice questions
exam simulations
visual concept diagrams
progress tracking
adaptive study recommendations

The platform should support exams such as NCLEX and REx-PN.
