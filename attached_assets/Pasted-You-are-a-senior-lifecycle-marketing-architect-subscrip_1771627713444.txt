You are a senior lifecycle marketing architect + subscription conversion strategist + full-stack engineer working on NurseNest.

GOAL:
Implement a segmented weekly study resource email system that:

- Provides genuine educational value
- Sends ONLY track-relevant content
- Builds NurseNest authority
- Encourages premium subscription conversion
- Uses psychologically optimized discount mechanics

CORE PRINCIPLE:
This is a clinical study support system, not a generic newsletter.

-----------------------------------
1) USER SEGMENTATION (MANDATORY)
-----------------------------------

On email signup, require selection of study track:

- RPN / LPN / PN
- RN
- NP
- Pre-Nursing / Student (optional)

- user.study_track

Allow editing later in settings.

-----------------------------------

-----------------------------------

-----------------------------------

-----------------------------------

Weekly email must feel like a curated learning resource.

Include:

- Professional intro
- Featured learning concept
- Clinical reasoning pearl
- Optional challenge / mini-quiz
- Links to related lessons/tools
- Soft conversion CTA

Tone:
Clinically credible, supportive, intelligent.

-----------------------------------
4) DISCOUNT / CONVERSION STRATEGY (CRITICAL)
-----------------------------------

Integrate discount mechanics designed for subscription stability.

PRIMARY OFFER:
Promote annual subscription discount.

Examples:
- “Annual Plan Study Commitment Discount”
- “Save X% on Yearly Access”

SECONDARY OFFER (optional):
Offer lower-friction 3-month starter discount.

Constraints:

- Discounts must feel educationally justified, not salesy
- Avoid aggressive marketing language
- Avoid perpetual discount abuse

-----------------------------------
5) DISCOUNT RULES
-----------------------------------

Implement logic:

- Unique discount codes per campaign or user segment
- Expiration windows (urgency without pressure)
- Prevent stacking or exploitation
- Track conversions attributed to email campaigns

-----------------------------------
6) AUTOMATION BEHAVIOR
-----------------------------------

Scheduler runs once weekly:

- Pull track-relevant content
- Avoid repetition
- Prefer high-engagement / high-value topics

-----------------------------------

❌ BUY NOW / LIMITED TIME
