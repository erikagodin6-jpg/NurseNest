Perform a full content and messaging audit of the NurseNest website and implement a structured FAQ system with cohesive wording across all sections.

Use the live site content as the source of truth and update wording where needed so the platform messaging is consistent.

Current problem:
The main NurseNest FAQ states that the platform is for nursing students only. However, the platform clearly supports nursing students, allied health professions, and new graduates.

This creates:
- inconsistent messaging
- confusion for users
- weaker SEO for allied health searches
- mismatch between homepage messaging and FAQ content

Goal:
Create a cohesive messaging structure across the entire site and implement separate FAQs for different user groups while keeping a unified platform explanation.

==================================================
PART 1 — UPDATE GLOBAL PLATFORM MESSAGING
==================================================

Audit wording across these pages:
- homepage
- FAQ
- pricing
- exam prep pages
- allied health pages
- new grad pages
- navigation descriptions
- hero sections
- SEO meta descriptions

Replace inconsistent statements such as:

“NurseNest helps nursing students prepare for exams.”

With cohesive language such as:

“NurseNest is a learning platform designed for nursing students, allied health professionals, and new graduates preparing for licensing exams, certifications, and clinical careers.”

Ensure this message is consistent across:
- hero copy
- FAQ
- About sections
- meta descriptions
- schema markup
- feature descriptions

Do not change the visual design while doing this.

1. Main Platform FAQ

3. New Grad FAQ

--------------------------------------------------

EXAM PREP

Ensure this page links to:
- respiratory therapy

- pharmacy technician

NEW GRAD FAQ
--------------------------------------------------

Create a separate FAQ for the New Grad Hub.

Focus on career transition questions.

Example sections:

NEW GRAD SUPPORT
- What is the NurseNest New Grad Hub?
- Is this included with exam prep?

CAREER PREPARATION

CERTIFICATIONS
