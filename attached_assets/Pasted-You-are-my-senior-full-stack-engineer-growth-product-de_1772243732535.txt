====================================================
SECTION 5 — ESTIMATED LIFT MODEL (ETHICAL + CONSISTENT)
====================================================

Do NOT hardcode fake lifts.

Instead:
- Use simulator endpoint to compute lift by applying deltas and comparing baseline P.
- For action lifts:
   - Map action → delta bundle:
       “Complete Module X” -> +3% domain accuracy in that topic + +100 questions exposure if paired quiz
       “SATA drill 20” -> +2% SATA accuracy and +20 question exposure
       “Strict mock exam” -> +1 strict mock and +small consistency boost

Compute lift = P_new − P_baseline.

Clamp lifts so single action cannot claim unrealistic jumps (>6% unless user is extremely low volume and trend is steep).
