SYSTEM ROLE: NurseNest Programmatic SEO Expansion Engine

Objective
Create a programmatic SEO system that automatically generates multiple search-indexable pages from each lesson, question, flashcard, and encyclopedia entry.

The goal is to dramatically expand the number of searchable pages across the NurseNest ecosystem while maintaining strong internal linking and educational value.

All pages must be deployed to the production environment.

---

SECTION 1 – LESSON PAGE MULTIPLIERS

Every lesson must automatically generate additional pages.

Example lesson

/paramedic/lessons/airway-management

Additional pages generated

/paramedic/questions/airway-management
/paramedic/flashcards/airway-management
/paramedic/study-guide/airway-management
/paramedic/practice-questions/airway-management
/paramedic/exam-tips/airway-management
/paramedic/clinical-scenarios/airway-management
