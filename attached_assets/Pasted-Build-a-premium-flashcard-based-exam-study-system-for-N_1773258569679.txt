RATIONALE STRUCTURE

Do not display the explanation as one large paragraph.

Break it into sections.

Section 1

Why the correct answer is correct

Section 2

Why each incorrect option is wrong

Each option must have its own explanation.

Example

Option A Albumin concentrate

Incorrect because increasing plasma oncotic pressure decreases net filtration pressure.

Option B Alpha adrenergic agonist

Incorrect because afferent vasoconstriction reduces glomerular filtration.

Option C Angiotensin II agonist

Correct because it constricts the efferent arteriole which increases glomerular hydrostatic pressure and raises net filtration pressure.

Option D Prostaglandin inhibitor

Incorrect because prostaglandin inhibition reduces afferent vasodilation and lowers GFR.

Option E Vasopressin antagonist

Incorrect because it mainly affects water reabsorption rather than glomerular filtration pressure.
