SECTION 2 — LICENSE MODEL LOGIC
=========================================================

COHORT MODEL:
- All students share accessEnd = semesterEndDate
- Enrollment stops after semesterEndDate
- Bulk enrollment via CSV
- Seat limit enforced

INDIVIDUAL MODEL:
- Each student has independent accessEnd
- accessEnd = examDate OR now + defaultDurationDays
- Rolling enrollment allowed
- Seat limit enforced

Switching models must require admin permission.
