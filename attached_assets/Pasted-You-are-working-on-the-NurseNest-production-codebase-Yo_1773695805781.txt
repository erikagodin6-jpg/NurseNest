BOW-TIE QUESTION STRUCTURE

Each Bow-Tie question must include:

Clinical Scenario
Patient Data
3 Columns:

1. Condition / Diagnosis
2. Actions / Interventions
3. Parameters to Monitor

Example:

Condition:
• Hypovolemia
• Sepsis
• Pulmonary embolism
• Anaphylaxis

Actions:
• Start IV fluids
• Administer oxygen
• Administer epinephrine
• Elevate legs

Parameters:
• Blood pressure
• Oxygen saturation
• Urine output
• Lactate

---

SATA QUESTION REQUIREMENTS

All SATA questions must:

• include 5–8 options
• contain 2–5 correct answers
• require clinical reasoning
• avoid obvious distractors

Topics should include:

• pharmacology
• prioritization
• safety
• pathophysiology
• nursing interventions
• lab interpretation
• herbal / naturopathic medication interactions

---

CASE STUDY STRUCTURE

Case studies must contain:

Patient profile
Assessment findings
Vital signs
Lab results
Medication list

Followed by **4–6 sequential questions** including:

• SATA
• Bow Tie
• Matrix
• MCQ
