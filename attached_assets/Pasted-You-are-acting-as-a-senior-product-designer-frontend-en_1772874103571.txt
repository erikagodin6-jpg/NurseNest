CONTEXT ABOUT THE PLATFORM
NurseNest is an online nursing education platform offering lessons, exam-style practice questions, flashcards, mock exams, and learning tools for nursing students preparing for licensing exams such as REx-PN, NCLEX-PN, NCLEX-RN, and nurse practitioner board exams. The site is designed to support both Canadian and U.S. nursing students.

The founder is Erika Godin, a practicing Registered Nurse who began her career as a Registered Practical Nurse (RPN). Because she started as an RPN and later became an RN, she personally went through multiple nursing licensing exam pathways. She is also currently pursuing a Master of Nursing degree. The platform has been in development for five years and was created after she experienced firsthand the difficulty of studying using platforms such as UWorld and Archer that primarily focus on American clinical standards, measurements, and lab values, which can create confusion for Canadian students preparing for exams like the REx-PN.

The platform aims to provide clinically accurate, organized, evidence-based nursing education resources designed for how nurses actually learn.
